About

Welcome to the Keweenaw Towns and Cities - Huoghton application. This is an interactive application where you can explore what the City of Houghton and the surrounding area looked like in the past through the use of historical maps and photographs. This application will show you the approximate location or focal point where the photograph was taken and the viewing area in which it covers.

Using the Application

Swipe left or right or press the left or right arrows to view photographs. The map will automatically update with a corresponding historical map and viewshed for each photograph.
